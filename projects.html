Smart Home Mobile App

High-Fidelity prototypes, wireframes, and sketches of the smart home mobile app. Designed with Jiwoo Lee, Matt Robinson, and Zack Clarke; we conducted marketing research, quantitive research (online surveys) and qualitative research (interviews), created the user personas, analyzed the users' problems (Kate and Matt completed these parts), drew the sketches (All team members completed this part), created wireframes (Jiwoo completed this part), and the high-fidelity prototypes (Kate completed this part).
